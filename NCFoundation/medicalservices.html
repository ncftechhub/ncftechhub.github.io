About Our Medical Center

Serving the Community Since 2021

In the heart of Namuwongo, one of Kampala's largest slum communities, the Namuwongo Community Foundation (NCF) identified a critical need for accessible healthcare services. In response, NCF established the Namuwongo Community Medical Service Centre, aiming to provide essential medical care to residents who previously faced insurmountable barriers to such services. The hospital's mission was clear: to offer free treatment to children and affordable care to adults, ensuring that financial constraints would not prevent anyone from receiving necessary medical attention.

Each day, the hospital's dedicated staff attended to 15 to 20 patients, addressing a range of health issues from common illnesses to more severe conditions. This consistent care became a cornerstone of the community, offering a reliable source of medical support where previously there was none. However, as time has progressed, the hospital has encountered significant challenges. Dwindling funds and limited support have severely impacted its operations, leading to a shortage of essential medicines and outdated equipment. These constraints have forced the hospital to scale back its services, now primarily offering pain relief, counselling, and referrals. Tragically, many community members cannot afford the fees charged by other hospitals, leaving them with limited options for their healthcare needs.

The potential closure of this vital facility poses a dire threat to the well-being of Namuwongo's residents. Recognizing the gravity of the situation, NCF has initiated a fundraiser to rejuvenate and sustain the hospital's operations. The goal is not only to restore the hospital's capacity to provide comprehensive medical care but also to enhance its services to better serve the community's evolving needs.

We earnestly appeal to the generosity of well-wishers, donors, and partners to support this critical endeavour. Contributions can take various forms: donations of medicines to replenish depleted supplies, sanitary pads to support the health and dignity of young girls, modern medical equipment to replace outdated apparatus, or voluntary services from medical professionals willing to lend their expertise. Each contribution, regardless of its nature or size, will play a pivotal role in revitalizing the hospital and ensuring its sustainability. The Namuwongo Community Medical Service Centre has long stood as a beacon of hope and healing in a community beset by numerous challenges. Its continued operation is not just about providing medical services; it's about affirming the dignity and worth of every individual in Namuwongo. By coming together to support this fundraiser, we can safeguard a vital lifeline for thousands and demonstrate the profound impact of collective compassion and action. Please join us in this crucial mission to save our beloved hospital. Your support will not only help restore essential medical services but also rekindle hope in the hearts of countless individuals who rely on the Namuwongo Community Medical Service Centre for their health and well-being.

Our Revitalization Project

Transforming Healthcare for Namuwongo

We're embarking on an ambitious $250,000 renovation project to modernize our facilities and expand our capacity to serve the community.

  • Expand our facility by 40% to reduce overcrowding
  • Modernize all medical equipment
  • Create dedicated isolation areas for infectious diseases
  • Improve sanitation and water systems
  • Train additional community health workers
